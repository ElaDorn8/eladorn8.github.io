About me:

I am a mostly self-taught Front-End Webdeveloper. "Mostly" because I did have some formal web-development classes while I was working on my Bachelors and Master's degree online. In those classes, I dabbled in Visual Basic and Visual C#. I have taught myself Javascript, HTML, and CSS through online courses and references. I am aware that there are spiffy things like bootstrap, that give you already coded elements for a website, but I prefer to make my own and reuse them (I feel it gives me more control over the element and I can do with it what I want without another learning curve). But if need be I can figure out how to get the "pre-coded" elements to do what I want.

In my spare time I like to make 3D models in Blender and playing around in Unity, or learn about AI/ML. I enjoy learning new things and I like staying current with technology.
